Track geometry · STANDARD v1.0.0 🔒
Canonical part geometry (semver 1.0.0) — constant by design so every part ever printed interoperates: slope 11.22° (30 mm drop per tile incl. the waterfall seam), curves R 143.6 mm (45 mm), channel 48 mm, supports on a 15 mm riser grid. Designs from files with other geometry are re-laid on this standard and flagged.
