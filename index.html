      Printable parts

      All parts export watertight (Manifold-verified), print-ready flat on the bed: track shells deck-up with washboard floors, waterfall dovetail seams and hex pillar sockets; figure parts lying on their sides for smooth hoof cams.

      Print settings (research-derived)

      Material: PLA (rigid → loud, sharp clack)
      Walls: 4–5 perimeters (toddler-proof)
      Track: as oriented, 10% gyroid infill, no supports — floor bridges the acoustic chamber
      Figure: parts lie on their sides — the printer draws the hoof cams as smooth continuous arcs
      Axle: 3 mm steel/brass rod (do NOT print it)
      Ballast: steel BBs in the bores, then glue every plug (choke-hazard seal)
